## Billing & Payment

### Payment Methods

- Credit Card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Billing via **Polar.sh**

### Billing Cycle

- **Monthly billing** only
- No annual contracts or commitments
- Cancel anytime (prorated refund for unused time)

### Overage Charges

**Develop:** $0.25 per 1,000 requests after 500k
**Growth:** $0.20 per 1,000 requests after 2M
